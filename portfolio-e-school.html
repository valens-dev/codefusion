project overview

eSchool is a cloud-based learning platform designed to offer a robust, secure, and integrated system for primary and secondary education institutions. Built using ASP.NET MVC and MSSQL Server technologies, eSchool centralizes the entire teaching process, from managing teaching performance and records to distributing materials and evaluating exams. The platform simplifies the management of student records, curricula, and staff, providing a seamless and user-friendly experience for students to learn, access resources, take exams, and monitor their academic progress from any computer or mobile device.

project features

eSchool incorporates several key modules to streamline the operations of educational institutions:

  • School Administrator: Facilitates the management of all school data, including curricula, preparation for the school year, and entry of teaching schedules, ensuring smooth administrative operations.
  • Office Module: Handles student registers, allows for the entry and access of student data, and publishes notices, documents, and reminders for students and teachers, enhancing communication and organization.
  • Class Teacher Module: Provides a comprehensive view of student records for each class, including basic data, subjects, grades, and teacher comments. It also allows for the entry of new students and management of student subjects.
  • Teacher Module: Offers insights into all subjects taught, records of classes held, student attendance and grades, setting teaching content and information, setting and reviewing assignments, communicating with students via a Q&A section, and creating and activating tests.
  • Student Module: Grants students access to all class-related data, notifications, class schedules, teaching materials, assignments, and tests, facilitating an organized and interactive learning experience.
  • Reports: Generates graphical and tabular reports on student performance, including the number of students, published teaching content, test statistics, and student success, as well as teacher activities, including published materials, assignments, and teacher evaluations.
  • Mobile Applications: Provides iOS and Android apps for students, allowing them to access the platform's features on the go, ensuring continuous engagement and accessibility.
project results

eSchool delivers substantial improvements in the management and educational experience of primary and secondary institutions. By integrating various administrative and teaching functions into a single cloud-based platform, eSchool enhances efficiency and accessibility for all users. The platform's intuitive interface and mobile applications ensure that students and staff can easily access and manage educational resources and data from anywhere. With comprehensive reporting capabilities, eSchool enables better tracking of student and teacher performance, facilitating data-driven decision-making. Overall, eSchool empowers educational institutions to provide a cohesive, efficient, and engaging learning environment, supporting academic excellence and streamlined administrative processes.
