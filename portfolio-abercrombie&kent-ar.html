project goals

Embark on a journey of luxury and adventure with Abercrombie & Kent, a pioneer in luxury travel for over 60 years. Founded by Geoffrey Kent and his parents, Valerie and Colonel John Kent, Abercrombie & Kent revolutionized the travel industry by opening up previously inaccessible destinations and setting a new standard for experiential travel. Today, Abercrombie & Kent stands as the world's premier luxury travel company, offering unforgettable experiences in over 100 countries across all seven continents. Our commitment to exceptional service, authentic local expertise, and exclusive insider access ensures that every journey with us is truly a journey of a lifetime.

project overview

Abercrombie & Kent boasts unparalleled global reach with over 55 offices in more than 30 countries, supported by a team of over 2,500 dedicated travel experts. Led by Resident Tour Directors® and local guides, our small-group journeys, Luxury Tailor Made Travel, Luxury Expedition Cruises, and Private Jet journeys offer unparalleled expertise and personalized service. Recognized as the gold standard for luxury travel, Abercrombie & Kent provides authentic discovery from a local perspective, combined with unparalleled style and service.

project results

Abercrombie & Kent's commitment to luxury and service is recognized worldwide, setting the industry standard year after year. Our dedication to excellence is reflected in our active memberships with prestigious associations, ensuring the highest standards of quality and integrity. Beyond delivering exceptional travel experiences, Abercrombie & Kent is deeply committed to sustainable tourism and community development. Through the A&K Philanthropy nonprofit network, spanning 24 countries across all seven continents, we invest in education, enterprise, health, and environmental initiatives to support and empower local communities and preserve the world's precious ecosystems. As we continue to celebrate travel as a force for good, Abercrombie & Kent remains at the forefront of luxury travel, delivering unparalleled experiences with a commitment to sustainability and social responsibility.
