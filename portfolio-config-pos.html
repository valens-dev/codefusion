project overview

ConfigPOS is an integrated application designed to streamline the management of orders, warehouse operations, and back-office administration in the catering industry. Utilizing technologies such as ASP.NET MVC and MSSQL Server, ConfigPOS offers a comprehensive software package that includes web, desktop, and mobile applications. This system ensures that restaurants, cafes, and other catering facilities can efficiently manage their business processes. With automatic updates and 24/7 support, ConfigPOS guarantees that users always have access to the latest features and continuous assistance. The web-based BackOffice administration is compliant with all legal regulations, providing complete control over operations from any internet-enabled device. Additionally, the mobile application delivers real-time reports, keeping users informed about their business activities on the go.

project features

ConfigPOS includes several robust modules to cater to the diverse needs of the catering industry:

  • POS Desktop Application: Manage orders and create invoices seamlessly. This module is essential for day-to-day operations, ensuring that orders are processed quickly and accurately.
  • Orders Mobile Application: This mobile app allows staff to define orders, which are then linked to the desktop application, bar, kitchen, and warehouse, streamlining the order management process.
  • BackOffice: Provides the capability to manage operations of multiple business units and companies from any device at any time, offering flexibility and control.
  • CONFIG Analysis: Offers real-time reports on sales data, staff efficiency, and consumption, providing valuable insights to enhance operational efficiency.
  • Lite: Includes back-office administration, invoicing, item management, defining offers and discounts, combining payment methods, shift management, multiple cash registers online, and support for touchscreens or keyboards.
  • Pro: Builds on the Lite version with additional functionalities such as warehouse traffic analysis, delivery management, support for multiple shops and legal entities, use of multiple price lists, table layout display, and staff time attendance tracking.
  • Ultimate: Includes all Pro features plus daily menus, combo sales, automatic price changes at defined intervals, complete material consumption analysis, and a suite of mobile applications (ConfigPOS Analysis, Android apps for delivery and dispatchers, chefs, and kitchen monitors).
project results

ConfigPOS delivers significant enhancements to the management and operational efficiency of catering facilities. By integrating order processing, warehouse management, and back-office administration into a single platform, ConfigPOS ensures that businesses can operate smoothly and effectively. The system's automatic updates and 24/7 support provide continuous improvements and assistance, while the mobile application keeps management informed with real-time data and reports. The various modules offer tailored solutions for different levels of operational needs, from basic order and invoicing management in the Lite version to comprehensive warehouse and delivery management in the Pro and Ultimate versions. Overall, ConfigPOS empowers catering businesses to deliver prompt service, maintain regulatory compliance, and optimize their operations for better performance and growth.
