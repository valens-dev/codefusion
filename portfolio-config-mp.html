project overview

ConfigMP is an integrated retail facility management application designed to streamline and enhance the operations of restaurants, cafes, and other catering establishments. Built with technologies such as ASP.NET, MVC, and MSSQL Server, ConfigMP provides comprehensive support for business processes through a suite of web, desktop, and mobile applications. Featuring automatic updates and 24/7 support, ConfigMP ensures users always have access to the latest features and continuous assistance. The platform's web-based BackOffice administration is compliant with all legal regulations, allowing full operational control from any internet-enabled device. Additionally, the mobile application delivers real-time reports, keeping users informed about their facilities' business activities on the go.

project features

ConfigMP offers a range of robust modules designed to meet the diverse needs of retail and catering facilities:

  • Sales: This module covers invoicing, special offers and discounts, combo sales of items, combining payment methods, and managing multiple price lists. It also includes automatic price changes and a loyalty program for customers, ensuring efficient and flexible sales operations.
  • Warehousing and Administration: This module provides comprehensive back-office administration, including adherence to standards, complete warehousing operations, and detailed analysis of warehouse traffic and conditions. It supports operations across multiple warehouses, enhancing inventory management and control.
  • Human Resources: This module manages shift work, staff working hours, and offers multiple ways for employees to log in to the system. It ensures efficient HR management, helping businesses keep accurate records and streamline workforce operations.
project results

ConfigMP significantly enhances the efficiency and operational control of retail and catering facilities. With its comprehensive sales module, businesses can manage invoicing, special offers, and customer loyalty programs seamlessly, ensuring a smooth sales process and improved customer satisfaction. The warehousing and administration module provides thorough oversight of inventory, allowing businesses to optimize their stock levels and reduce waste. The human resources module simplifies workforce management, ensuring accurate tracking of staff hours and efficient shift scheduling. ConfigMP's web-based BackOffice and mobile application enable real-time monitoring and management, ensuring businesses stay compliant with legal regulations and can make informed decisions based on up-to-date data. Overall, ConfigMP empowers businesses to deliver better service, improve operational efficiency, and drive growth.
